This is the official webpage for UCL Undergraduate Mathematics Colloquium.

The colloquium is a weekly talks series aimed at maths undergraduates on maths-related research or other interesting maths topics.


It is is currently hosted by Tatiana Kurlina and Tin Wong.

Previous hosts include Travor Liu, R. Zhang, Jon Im.


We can be contacted by Email, or you can contact us via any of the following social media platforms: YouTube, Instagram.
